Cancer Drug Interaction Database

AS.410.701 - Final Project

Welcome! This is a searchable database of cancer drugs and how well they interact with different cancer cell lines. The drug-cell interaction is measured from IC50, or the concentration of drug required to inhibit 50% of cell growth. The lower the IC50, the more effective the drug is at inhibiting cell growth (i.e. you need a lower concentration of drug to inhibit the cell growth).
